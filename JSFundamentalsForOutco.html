Javascript fundamentals

Allow me to explain the application process, one would submit an application, we review your background. If its a match you would be invited to do a online coding interview. Based on performance you would either be granted admission or given feedback on what is needed to improve.

The fundamentals check is a one-hour Google Hangout scheduled at your convenience with one of our staff members. During that hour, we'll spend 5-15 minutes getting to know you a bit better in terms of your goals, what you think you'd most benefit from with the program, etc. We'll then dive in to 30-45 minutes of pair programming, where we'll check your ability to write functions, manipulate strings, and/or traverse arrays in the language of your choice with some simple problems guided by our staff member. We're not looking for the right answer necessarily, just to ensure that you have a strong grasp of your language's syntax, basic operations, and foundational data structures. The last 5-15 minutes will be reserved for feedback and any questions you might have.
